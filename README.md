**🍔 QuickBite Express: Crisis & Recovery Analytics Dashboard**

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze business performance during a crisis period across revenue, operations, customer behavior, and city-level impact—enabling data-driven recovery strategies.

**📌 2. Short Description / Purpose**

The QuickBite Express Dashboard analyzes how a food delivery business was impacted during a crisis by comparing pre-crisis vs crisis performance. It helps identify revenue loss, operational inefficiencies, customer churn, and city-level risks to support strategic recovery decisions.

**🛠️ 3. Tech Stack**

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Main platform for interactive dashboard creation
• 📂 Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation, and shaping
• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI calculations, measures, and dynamic insights
• 🧩 Data Modeling – Relationships between orders, customers, cities, and restaurants
• 📁 Excel / SQL – Data source and preprocessing
• 📸 File Format – .pbix (development), .png (preview images)

**📊 4. Data Source**

Source: Simulated business dataset (Food delivery operations)

The dataset includes:

Orders data (pre-crisis & crisis)
Revenue & cancellation metrics
Delivery performance (time, SLA breaches, delays)
Customer behavior (frequency, churn, AOV)
City & restaurant-level performance

**🚀 5. Features / Highlights**
**🔴 Business Problem**

Food delivery businesses often face sudden disruptions (like operational crises), leading to:

Sharp revenue decline
Increased cancellations & delays
Customer churn and reduced engagement
City-specific performance drops

Raw data alone makes it difficult to quickly identify root causes and recovery opportunities.

**🎯 Goal of the Dashboard**

To build an interactive analytics tool that:

Compares Pre-Crisis vs Crisis performance
Identifies key risk areas across operations, customers, and cities
Helps stakeholders take data-driven recovery actions
Provides a 360° view of business health

**💡 Business Impact & Insights**

• 📉 Revenue dropped ~70%, driven by order decline (~69%)
• ⚠️ SLA breaches surged (23% → 83%), indicating operational breakdown
• 🚫 Customer churn reached 87%, showing major retention issues
• 🏙️ Tier-1 cities contributed most to revenue loss
• ⏱️ Delivery delays and cancellations strongly correlated with negative reviews

**🧠 Key Takeaways**
Operations failure → Customer dissatisfaction → Revenue loss
Delivery performance is a critical driver of retention
High-risk cities need targeted recovery strategies
Improving SLA compliance can directly reduce churn
